# Turning Evidence into Everyday Health Practice

The **Practice Knowledge System (PKS)** is an evidence-based knowledge platform dedicated to translating scientific research into practical guidance for better health.

Our aim is to bridge the gap between research and real-world practice by providing trustworthy, understandable, and actionable information for clinicians, students, health professionals, patients, caregivers, and the general public.

Rather than replacing medical consultation, the Practice Knowledge System supports informed decision-making by bringing together the best available evidence with practical implementation guidance.

## What You Will Find

### Health Topics

Evidence-based information organised by disease or health condition, including:

* Background and causes
* Risk factors
* Lifestyle approaches
* Evidence summaries
* Practical recommendations
* References to scientific literature

### Lifestyle Interventions

Practical guidance on interventions that promote health, including:

* Yoga
* Nutrition
* Physical activity
* Sleep
* Stress management
* Behaviour change
* Mind–body practices

### Evidence Library

Concise summaries of scientific evidence designed for rapid learning.

These include:

* Evidence Briefs
* Systematic Review Summaries
* Practice Perspectives
* Research Notes

### Clinical Practice Guides

Resources designed to assist clinicians and healthcare providers in translating evidence into practice.

These guides emphasise:

* Patient-centred care
* Behaviour change
* Lifestyle medicine
* Shared decision-making
* Practical implementation

### Knowledge Hub

Broader discussions on topics that influence health, including:

* Prevention
* Lifestyle Medicine
* Public Health
* Health Systems
* Integrative Care
* Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

## Editorial Principles

The Practice Knowledge System is guided by five principles:

1. Evidence before opinion.
2. Transparency in sources and references.
3. Practical applicability.
4. Continuous updating as evidence evolves.
5. Respect for scientific uncertainty where evidence is limited.

## Intended Audience

This platform is designed for:

* Patients and caregivers seeking trustworthy health information.
* Clinicians integrating lifestyle approaches into practice.
* Students learning evidence-based healthcare.
* Researchers interested in knowledge translation.
* Policymakers and public health professionals.

## Our Vision

We believe that scientific discoveries improve health only when they are translated into everyday practice.

The Practice Knowledge System is one component of a broader effort to bridge the evidence-to-health gap through knowledge translation, implementation, and continuous learning.

Every page on this site is intended to answer one practical question:

**"How can the best available evidence be applied to improve health?"**
